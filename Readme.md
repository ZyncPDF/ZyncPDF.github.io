

# ZyncPDF: An Open-Source, Privacy-Focused PDF WebApp

ZyncPDF is an **Open-Source, Privacy-Focused Alternative** to popular WebApps such as ILovePDF, SmallPDF, PDFCandy, and FreeConvert. We offer **30+ Tools**, all **Free**, with **No Ads**, **No File Size Limits**, **Unlimited Batch Processing**, **No Registration Needed**, & **Unlimited Usage**. We make it easy for anyone to modify their PDF files efficiently and with privacy in mind.

We developed ZyncPDF with two things in mind: **Privacy** and **User Experience**. We made ZyncPDF to be completely, **100% client-side**, meaning your PDF files are **never uploaded to any server ever**. We can't and will never see what files you use on our WebApp. We also don't sell your data, have ads, or track you in any way.

We are committed to your privacy and strive to limit tracking by avoiding unnecessary data collection wherever possible. ZyncPDF was also built to be as code-efficient as possible, allowing you to convert & modify your PDF files efficiently. We are funded by only two things: **Sponsors** & **Voluntary Donators**. We believe this approach is the best way to fund our WebApp without having a paywall or ads that track your data, while being able to afford keeping our WebApp running.

## Tech Stack & Libraries

ZyncPDF is built with modern, efficient technologies to ensure fast and reliable performance:

- **HTML, CSS, JavaScript** — No frameworks required, pure vanilla code
- **PDF-Lib** — In-browser PDF creation and editing
- **Mozilla PDF.js** — Rendering PDFs for previews and thumbnails
- **JSZip** — ZIP packaging for multi-file downloads
- **Pica** — High-quality image resizing
- **FileReader** — Local file handling and processing

All libraries are loaded from trusted CDNs and work entirely client-side.
