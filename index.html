Frequently Asked Questions

Is ZyncPDF really free forever?

Yes! ZyncPDF is completely free with no premium tiers, no usage limits, and no hidden costs.

How does ZyncPDF protect my privacy?

All processing happens directly in your browser. Your files are never uploaded to any server.

Do I need to create an account?

No registration, no email, no personal information required. Just open the tool and start converting instantly.

What file formats are supported?

We support PDF, PNG, JPEG, JPG, TXT, HTML, Markdown, Word, Excel, PowerPoint, HEIF, WEBP, SVG, and many more formats.
