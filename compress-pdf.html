Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is it safe to compress my PDF files here?

Absolutely. All processing happens in your browser, so your files never leave your device. We do not upload or store any of your documents, ensuring complete privacy.

How much will my file be compressed?

The compression level depends on the content of your PDF. Files with many images will see a more significant size reduction than text-only documents. Our tool aims to provide the best balance between file size and quality.

Does compressing a PDF reduce its quality?

Our tool uses advanced algorithms to reduce file size with minimal impact on quality. For most documents, any quality change is unnoticeable, especially for text. Image quality is slightly reduced to achieve smaller file sizes, but it is optimized for on-screen viewing.
