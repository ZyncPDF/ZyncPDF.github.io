Why use our Image Compression tool?

  • 100% Private: Compression happens locally in your browser. Your files never leave your device.

  • Smaller Files, Great Quality: Reduce image size while preserving visual clarity.

  1. Drop your .jpg/.jpeg/.png files into the area above or click to choose them.
  2. Optionally adjust compression settings if available.
  3. Click "Process Files" to compress.
  4. Download your optimized images from the results section.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Are my images uploaded to a server?

No. Everything happens in your browser. We never upload or collect your files.

Which formats are supported?

JPEG and PNG are supported by this tool.

Will the image resolution change?

Compression primarily reduces file size while maintaining the same resolution, unless you choose resizing options if available.
