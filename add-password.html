How to add a password to your PDF:

  1. Step 1: Drag and drop your PDF into the upload area, or click to select it.
  2. Step 2: Enter and confirm the new password in the options box.
  3. Step 3: Click "Process Files" to encrypt your PDF.
  4. Step 4: Download your new, password-protected PDF.
