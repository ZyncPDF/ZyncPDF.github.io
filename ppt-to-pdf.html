Why use our PowerPoint to PDF tool?

  • High Quality: Renders slides at high quality for crisp text and images.
  • Automatic Orientation: Each slide's orientation is set automatically based on its aspect ratio.
  • 100% Private: Runs entirely in your browser. Files never leave your device.
  • Free & Open Source: No registration, ads, or limits.
  • Batch Conversion: Convert multiple presentations at once.

Notes about PPT vs PPTX

  • PPTX Supported: This tool renders PPTX (modern PowerPoint) directly in your browser.
  • PPT Legacy: Legacy PPT files may not render. If a PPT fails, open it in PowerPoint and "Save As" PPTX, then try again.

How to use the PowerPoint to PDF tool:

  1. Step 1: Drag and drop your .ppt or .pptx files into the upload area, or click to select them.
  2. Step 2: Click the "Process Files" button to start the conversion.
  3. Step 3: Once processed, download your new PDF(s) from the results section.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Do I need to pick orientation?

No. Orientation is applied automatically per slide based on its aspect ratio.

Are my presentations uploaded?

No. Everything happens in your browser. We never upload or collect your files.

Will animations or transitions be preserved?

No. The output is a static PDF. Visual slide content is preserved, but dynamic effects are not.
