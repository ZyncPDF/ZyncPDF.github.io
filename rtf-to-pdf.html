Why use our RTF to PDF tool?

  • High Quality: Uses rtf.js to render RTF content, then captures to PDF with html2canvas + jsPDF.
  • 100% Private: Runs entirely in your browser. Files never leave your device.
  • Free & Open Source: No registration, ads, or limits.
  • Batch Conversion: Convert multiple RTF files in one go.

How to use the RTF to PDF tool:

  1. Step 1: Drag and drop your .rtf files into the upload area, or click to select them.
  2. Step 2: Click the "Process Files" button to start the conversion.
  3. Step 3: Once processed, download your new PDF(s) from the results section.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Are my RTF files uploaded to a server?

No. Everything happens in your browser. We never upload or collect your files.

Will the formatting be preserved?

We use rtf.js for parsing and high-quality rendering. Most text and layout are preserved well, though very complex documents may need minor adjustments.

Can I convert multiple RTF files at once?

Yes, batch conversion is supported. Add multiple files before processing.
