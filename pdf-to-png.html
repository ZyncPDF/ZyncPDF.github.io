Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is it safe to convert my PDF to PNG here?

Absolutely. All processing happens in your browser, so your files never leave your device. We do not upload or store any of your documents, ensuring complete privacy.

What is the quality of the output PNG images?

Our tool converts PDF pages to high-resolution PNG images, preserving the clarity and detail of your original document.

Can I convert a password-protected PDF?

For security reasons, our tool cannot process password-protected or encrypted PDF files. Please remove the protection before uploading.
