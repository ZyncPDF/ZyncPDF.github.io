Privacy & Data

Control how your usage data is stored. All data stays in your browser — nothing is ever sent to a server.

Incognito Mode by Default

History and favorites are disabled until you explicitly enable them. This is the most private option.

Enable Local History & Favorites

Store tool usage history and favorites in IndexedDB.

Auto-Delete History

Automatically remove history entries after a time period.

What gets stored?

  • Favorites: Tool IDs and names you heart.
  • History: Tool IDs and timestamps of visits.
  • NOT stored: Files, text inputs, images, or any personal data.
  • NOT transmitted: Nothing leaves your browser. No analytics, no tracking pixels.
